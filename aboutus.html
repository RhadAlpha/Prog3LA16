About Us

Menu-do is all about Filipino dishes with tasty recipes and inexpensive ingredients. Since 2018, our team has been publishing Filipino recipes. We started first from our restaurant business before this website was established, which inspired us to share our delectable recipes. Our goal is to teach people to be better in the kitchen and how to cook Filipino dishes in the simplest way.
