BISTEK RECIPE

5 | 659 People

A traditional Filipino meal called bistek Tagalog is made of thinly sliced beef that is stewed in a blend of citrus juices.


PREP TIME COOKING TIME TOTAL TIME SERVINGS
1 hour 40 minutes 1 Hr and 40 Mins 6

INGREDIENTS:


  • 1 1/2 lbs beef sirloin thinly sliced
  • 5 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 4 pieces calamansi or 1-piece lemon
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 3 cloves garlic minced
  • 3 pieces yellow onion sliced into rings
  • 4 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 pinch salt


DIRECTIONS:


STEP 1. Marinate beef in soy sauce, lemon (or calamansi), and ground black pepper for at least 1 hour. Note: marinate overnight for best result

STEP 2. Heat the cooking oil in a pan then pan-fry half of the onions until the texture becomes soft. Set aside

STEP 3. Drain the marinade from the beef. Set it aside. Pan-fry the beef on the same pan where the onions were fried for 1 minute per side. Remove from the pan. Set aside

STEP 4. Add more oil if needed. Saute garlic and remaining raw onions until onion softens.

STEP 5. Add more oil if needed. Saute garlic and remaining raw onions until onion softens.

STEP 6. Add beef. Cover the pan and simmer until meat is tender. Note: Add water as needed.

STEP 7. Season with ground black pepper and salt as needed. Top with pan-fried onions. Transfer to a serving plate. Serve hot. Share and Enjoy!
