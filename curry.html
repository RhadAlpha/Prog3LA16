Pinoy Chicken Curry RECIPE

5 | 700 People

To prepare Pinoy Chicken Curry, a blend of curry powder, coconut milk, and optional fish sauce is used.


PREP TIME COOKING TIME TOTAL TIME SERVINGS
10 minutes 1 hour 1 hour 10 minutes 6

INGREDIENTS:


  • 2 lbs. chicken cut into serving pieces
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 piece potato cubed
  • 4 cloves garlic minced
  • 2 stalks celery sliced
  • 1 piece red bell pepper sliced
  • 2 pieces long green pepper
  • 1 piece onion chopped
  • 2 thumbs ginger cut into strips
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose cream optional
  • 1 cup water
  • fish sauce and ground black pepper to taste


DIRECTIONS:


STEP 1. Heat oil in a pan. Fry potato for 1 minute per side. Remove from the pan. Set aside.

STEP 2. Using the remaining oil, saute garlic, onion, celery, and ginger until onion softens.

STEP 3. Add the chicken pieces. Saute until the outer part of the chicken turns light brown.

STEP 4. Add 1 tablespoon fish sauce. Continue saut√©ing for 1 minute.

STEP 5. Pour-in coconut milk and water. Let boil.

STEP 6. Add curry powder. Stir until the powder is completely diluted. Cover the pot and continue cooking between low to medium heat until the liquid reduces to half.

STEP 7. Add the red bell pepper and pan fried-potato. Cook for 5 minutes.

STEP 8. Season with fish sauce and ground black pepper as needed. You can also add all-purpose cream if desired. Transfer to a serving bowl. Serve and enjoy!
