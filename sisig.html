Pork Sisig RECIPE

5 | 559 People

A popular Filipino meal is pork sisig. It may be regarded as a starter or a main course. Pork mince, ears, and the face of the pig are the key components of this meal.


PREP TIME COOKING TIME TOTAL TIME SERVINGS
12 minutes 1 hour 30 minutes 1 hour 42minutes 6

INGREDIENTS:


  • 1 lb. pig ears
  • 1 1/2 lb pork belly
  • 1 piece onion minced
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 knob ginger minced (optional)
  • 3 tablespoons chili flakes
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 piece lemon or 3 to 5 pieces calamansi
  • ½ cup butter or margarine
  • ¼ lb chicken liver
  • 6 cups water
  • 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 piece egg (optional)


DIRECTIONS:


STEP 1. Pour the water in a pan and bring to a boil Add salt and pepper.

STEP 2. Put-in the pigs ears and pork belly then simmer for 40 minutes to 1 hour (or until tender).

STEP 3. Remove the boiled ingredients from the pot then drain excess water

STEP 4. Grill the boiled pig ears and pork belly until done

STEP 5. Chop the pig ears and pork belly into fine pieces

STEP 6. In a wide pan, melt the butter or margarine. Add the onions. Cook until onions are soft.

STEP 7. Put-in the ginger and cook for 2 minutes

STEP 8. Add the chicken liver. Crush the chicken liver while cooking it in the pan.

STEP 9. Add the chopped pig ears and pork belly. Cook for 10 to 12 minutes

STEP 10. Put-in the soy sauce, garlic powder, and chili. Mix well

STEP 11. Add salt and pepper to taste

STEP 12. Put-in the mayonnaise and mix with the other ingredients

STEP 13. Transfer to a serving plate. Top with chopped green onions and raw egg.

STEP 14. Serve hot. Share and Enjoy (add the lemon or calamansi before eating)
