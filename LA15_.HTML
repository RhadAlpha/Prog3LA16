CHICKEN ADOBO RECIPE

5 | 659 People

Classic chicken adobo dish that's easy to make and appreciated by everyone who tries it.

It's a little saucier than classic adobo and tastes great served over rice.


PREP TIME COOKING TIME TOTAL TIME SERVINGS
10 Minutes 1 Hour 1 Hr and 10 Mins 6

INGREDIENTS:


  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 (3 pound) chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, quartered and sliced
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • ⅔ cup low sodium soy sauce
  • ⅓ cup white vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons black pepper
  • 1 bay leaf


DIRECTIONS:


STEP 1. In a large pan over medium-high heat, heat the vegetable oil. Cook the chicken until golden brown, about 2 to 3 minutes per side. Place the chicken on a platter and set aside.

STEP 2. Cook until the onion and garlic are cooked and golden, about 6 minutes.

STEP 3. Season with garlic powder, black pepper, and bay leaf and pour in the soy sauce and vinegar.

STEP 4. Return the chicken to the pan, crank up the heat to high, and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to medium-low, cover, and cook for 40 to 50 minutes, or until the chicken is soft and cooked through.
